How FlowCredit Works

A transparent, on-chain revenue-based lending protocol that aligns incentives between borrowers and lenders through immutable smart contracts on Creditcoin.

The Problem

Traditional lending markets are built on collateral and equity. Banks demand hard assets—real estate, inventory, or personal guarantees—leaving entrepreneurs and businesses in emerging markets trapped outside the financial system. For those without significant collateral, the only alternative is equity funding, which dilutes ownership and transfers control to investors.

Even when collateral exists, loan approval timelines stretch across weeks or months. Credit decisions rely on opaque scoring systems, geographic biases, and subjective risk assessments. This friction excludes billions of people from accessing capital when they need it most. The emerging markets face an acute capital access gap: entrepreneurs with viable businesses cannot borrow because they lack the right paperwork, location, or relationship.

FlowCredit reimagines lending by tying repayment directly to business revenue. Instead of pledging collateral or surrendering equity, borrowers share a percentage of future revenue until a repayment cap is reached. This aligns incentives: lenders succeed only if the borrower succeeds. Speed increases. Barriers drop. Financial inclusion becomes real.

Smart Contract Mechanics

Real-World Example

Scenario: A borrower raises 1,000 USDT, agrees to a 5% revenue share, and sets a repayment cap of 1.3x (130% of principal).

Repayment Flow: Each month, the borrower reports revenue and automatically shares 5% with the lender through the smart contract. Payments accrue until the total reaches 1,300 USDT. Once this cap is reached, the loan is fully repaid and the revenue share stops.

Why It Works: The borrower's repayment burden scales with their success. During slow months, fewer funds are owed. During growth periods, repayment accelerates naturally. The lender's returns are aligned with borrower prosperity, eliminating the disconnect in traditional debt where lenders profit from borrower distress.

No Over-Repayment

The contract enforces a precise repayment cap: principal plus agreed revenue share. Once cumulative payments reach this cap, the escrow automatically stops accepting further payments. The borrower never pays a dollar more than the immutable loan terms permit—a guarantee written directly into the blockchain.

Immutable Loan Terms

Loan parameters—amount, revenue share percentage, duration, and repayment cap—are written into the smart contract bytecode at creation. No admin key, no upgrade, no governance backdoor can alter them after funding. These terms are publicly visible on-chain and enforced by the EVM, giving all parties deterministic, tamper-proof guarantees.

Why Creditcoin

Creditcoin is purpose-built for real-world lending infrastructure, making it the ideal foundation for FlowCredit's revenue-based model.

Real-World Credit Infrastructure

Creditcoin is purpose-built to record and verify real-world credit transactions, bridging traditional finance with decentralized lending.

Emerging Markets First

Designed for cross-border lending in emerging economies, with identity and credit history primitives that serve underbanked entrepreneurs and businesses.

Cross-Chain Interoperability

Native bridges enable FlowCredit to settle on Creditcoin while accepting capital from multiple blockchains, lowering global barriers to entry.

Decentralized Governance

Creditcoin's independent validator set and community governance ensure lending markets remain neutral and free from centralized control.

Scalable & Cost-Efficient

With fast finality and minimal fees, Creditcoin enables high-frequency revenue payments without prohibitive transaction costs.

Privacy & Compliance Ready

Built-in identity and whitelisting primitives support regulatory readiness for institutions entering emerging market lending.

Security Model

FlowCredit prioritizes transparency, immutability, and non-custodial design to protect borrowers and lenders alike.

Non-Custodial Design

Funds are held and enforced by the smart contract escrow, not by a centralized intermediary.

Audited Smart Contracts

Every loan is secured by deterministic escrow logic deployed on-chain.

Immutable Terms

Loan parameters are frozen at creation—no governance backdoors, no parameter changes, no discretionary modifications after funding.

Public Verifiability

All loan activity is recorded on-chain. Any party can independently verify balances, payment history, and contract behavior.

Deterministic Execution

Repayments and distributions are triggered by transparent, time-based mechanisms and verifiable revenue reports, leaving zero room for discretion.

Transparent Revenue Oracles

Revenue data feeds are decentralized and publicly auditable, ensuring lenders and borrowers trust the same source of truth.

Future Vision

  • Native Business Integration — Direct connectors to accounting software, payment gateways, and e-commerce platforms for automated revenue sharing.
